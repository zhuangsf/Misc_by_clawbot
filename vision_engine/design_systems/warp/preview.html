Elevation & Depth

Remarkably flat — depth through borders and photography, not shadows.

Level 0 — Flat
No shadow. The warm dark canvas baseline. Most surfaces rest here.
Level 1 — Veil
Ultra-subtle white overlay (rgba(255,255,255,0.04)). Barely visible differentiation.
Level 2 — Border
Semi-transparent border (rgba(226,226,226,0.35)). Ghostly containment for cards.
Level 3 — Ambient
Subtle ambient shadow for floating elements. Used sparingly.
