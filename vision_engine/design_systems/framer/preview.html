Elevation & Depth

Inverted elevation system — blue ring shadows for containment, white edge highlights for floating elements.

Level 0 — Flat
No shadow. Pure black surface. The void baseline that everything else rises from.
Level 1 — Ring
Framer Blue glow ring (rgba(0,153,255,0.15) 1px). Card borders, interactive outlines.
Level 2 — Contained
Near-black ring (rgb(9,9,9) 2px). Subtle containment on dark surfaces.
Level 3 — Floating
White top-edge highlight + deep ambient shadow (30px spread). Elevated cards, modals, dropdowns.
